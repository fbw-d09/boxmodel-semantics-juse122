Game features

  • Spend the days as a normal student: go to school, hang out with friends, hold a part-time job
  • After school, enter the "Palaces" of people's hearts and root out the evil within
  • Your bonds are your power as "Confidants" provide you strength and aid in battle and beyond!
Source: Persona 5 PS4 game box shot

Story

A group of high school students are out to reform Tokyo society. Troubled by the deception and hypocrisy all around them, these Phantom Thieves will steal into the hearts of people and confront the problem from the inside out!
